My Journey

My journey into coding/programming has been an interesting one as I'm sure many can relate to. My passion towards problem solving and building originally led me into a career of Interior Design. Being an ID I could work directly with Architects to design the built environment and see how humans and the environment interact with it. However, I found myself more enthralled with the technologies we used to build more efficiently and sustainbly. I started thinking about how these technologies & softwares were designed and built and how they could be improved. Then the pandemic hit and made me realize (like most people) how fragile some industries are. That's when I started thinking more seriously about a career as a Software Engineer and how I could merge my passion for sustainability, problem solving, and designing/building into the world of tech.
